ABOUT ALEXANDER J. BROWN

I am Alexander Brown, and I'm a college junior. This is my second semester here at SIUE as a transfer student from Southwestern Illinois College in Belleville. I love to read, play music, or watch TV to kill time. Other times, I'm an avid gamer, so I play any game that isn't fantasy-heavy or horror. Everything else I would be open to playing. As for film, I love all kinds of films, and studying film aspects and how it has evolved through the eras, and over the last centuries of motion picture and animated works as well. Currently I am majoring in Mass Communications with a focus in media production. I'm big on film and TV broadcasting, but I wouldn't say no to any other opportunity that comes with that. I think it would be interesting to learn more about the media, the ins and outs of it all, and how it affects society as a whole.

Personally, I hope to learn some coding. This is something I did last semester with Python and Java, and a bit of HTML in high school. But considering this course may be code-heavy, I would like to learn Dreamweaver and CSS, so anything that can help me succeed in the course, that would be great!
